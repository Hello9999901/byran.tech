DeLorean DMC-12

A cool, sci-fi-themed DeLorean from the famous movie Back To the Future
It is the original version, not the customized one in the movie. This is mainly topology and shading practice; if you do Blender, you know how important that is. I did not do the interior; I focused on the exterior geometry. I thought it turned out pretty good.
Made by me, inspired by the DeLorean DMC-12. P.S.: It is meant to look fake/way to perfect to be in this world.
